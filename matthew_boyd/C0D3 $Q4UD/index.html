Code Squad is a Harlem based tech course located at your local, Harlem Business Alliance. The chosen participants will undergo an instensive programming course for the next 10 weeks. By the end of the cycle, the students will develop the skills to not only code a website, they will intregret the knowledge to build apps, front door code based language, and other frontiers which in hopes will have an impact in our community. In collaboration with the course and the knowledge that will be obtained, the students will learn how to work together effieciently. Through completion of varies challenges and projects given from instructors, participants of Code Squad will be to thrive and adapt to the many difficulties that await at not only the workspace, the tech frontier as well.
